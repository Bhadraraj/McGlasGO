OUR STORY

McGLASGO Manufacturing Research excels by seamlessly integrating research and manufacturing under one roof, featuring a modern scientific center and a well-equipped manufacturing unit at Coimbatore, Tamilnadu state. Established in 2020 with a team of highly qualified scientists and industrial specialists, all of whom have garnered global recognition, the company holds ISO 9001:2015 certification. This visionary company not only resolves issues but also sets new standards for innovation and global success through collaborative efforts with seasoned scientists and cutting-edge technologies.

McGlasgo aspires to redefine the construction industry, crafting unparalleled, high-quality products with the collaboration of international scientists, setting a new standard for excellence and innovation

Empowering the construction industry, McGlasgo's mission is to engineer excellence through the fusion of cutting-edge scientific knowledge, delivering unparalleled, high-quality construction products. Our commitment extends to creating distinctive solutions tailored for Indian buildings, where durability and reliability are never compromised. Trust McGlasgo for a legacy of precision and quality in every project
